## üîß Featured Projects

- **[combatlearn](https://github.com/EttoreRocchi/combatlearn)** ‚Äî Scikit-learn compatible implementation of the `ComBat` algorithm for batch effect correction in high-dimensional data. *Useful for harmonizing high-throughput data across studies.*

- **[ResPredAI](https://github.com/EttoreRocchi/ResPredAI)** ‚Äî Machine learning pipeline for predicting antimicrobial resistance from demographic and clinical data. *[Artificial Intelligence model to predict resistances in Gram-negative bloodstream infections](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41746-025-01696-x) by Bonazzetti et al. (2025).*

- **[MaldiAMRKit](https://github.com/EttoreRocchi/MaldiAMRKit)** ‚Äî Toolkit to read and preprocess MALDI-TOF mass spectra for AMR analyses.

- **[CATS](https://github.com/Physics4MedicineLab/CATS)** ‚Äî A tool to compare Cas9 nucleases by detecting overlapping PAM sites and enabling allele-specific targeting of disease mutations, with ClinVar integration and a user-friendly interface. *[CATS: a bioinformatic tool for automated Cas9 nucleases activity comparison in clinically relevant contexts](https://doi.org/10.3389/fgeed.2025.1571023) by Rocchi et al. (2025).*

- **[CAMISIM-BrokenStick](https://github.com/Physics4MedicineLab/CAMISIM-BrokenStick)** ‚Äî Extension of `CAMISIM` to simulate metagenomic sequencing data using a broken stick model. *Enables simulation of metagenomic data with controllable evolutionary dynamics of microbial communities.*

- **[APOBECSeeker](https://github.com/Physics4MedicineLab/APOBECSeeker)** ‚Äî Pipeline for the identification of APOBEC-style mutations from multiple sequence alignment. *[Understanding the evolutionary dynamics of Monkeypox virus through less explored pathways](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-025-11855-5) by De Pascali et al. (2025).*
